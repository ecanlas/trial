Year Up is consisted of 11 months of Intensive I.T. Training and Professional Development

Year Up is a one-year, intensive training program that provides low-income young adults, ages 18-24, with a combination of hands-on skill development, college credits, corporate internships, and support.

TECHNICAL SKILLS CLASSES:

  • Desktop and Network Support

  • Help Desk

  • Hardware Repair

  • Operating Systems

  • Disk Formatting, Partitioning and Ghosting

  • Peripherals

  • Software Installation

  • Viruses and Malware

  • Microsoft Office and Outlook

  • Networking and TCP/IP

  • Investment Operations

  • Customer Service

  • Fund Accounting

PROFESSIONAL SKILLS CLASSES:

  • Business Writing Skills

  • Time Management

  • Career Networking

  • Communicating Clearly and Effectively

  • Conflict Resolution and Negotiation

  • Working in Teams

  • Presentation Skills

  • Workplace Norms

  • Introduction to Business

  • Personal Finance

  • Workplace Legal Issues

  • Work/Life Balance

Year Up Bay Area is a one-year, intensive training program that provides low-income young adults, ages 18-24, with a combination of hands-on skill development, college credits, and corporate internships. Since opening our doors in 2008, we have helped over 500 young adults cross the Opportunity Divide in the San Francisco Bay Area. 88% of our graduates are employed (earning competitive wages) or are attending college within four months of completing the program.
